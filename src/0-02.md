sentence: She became an actress.
syntax: SVC

structure:
S: She
V: became
C: an actress

tags:

- [SVC]
- [linking_verb]
- [subject_complement]

decision:

1. became はリンク動詞（状態変化を表す）
2. an actress は主語 She を説明している（主格補語）
3. よって SVC

rule:

1. be系ならほぼ無条件でSVC
2. become / seem / appear も高確率でSVC
3. 感覚動詞（look/feel/sound/taste/smell）は
   → 「主語の状態」ならSVC
   → 「動作対象がある」ならSVO
4. get / turn / grow / stay / remain は
   → 状態変化ならSVC

- S = C は“結果”であって“判定条件”ではない

mistake:

- 「S=CならSVC」は不正確
  - 例: They elected him president. → SVOC（CはOを説明）
  - 例: She made him happy. → SVOC
  - 例: I consider him smart. → SVOC
